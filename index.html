from farm to fork

We aim to make the world a more transparent place by tracking your product from farm to fork.

About us

With over 200 independent farmers all over Britain who are dedicated to ensure that their cruelty-free, organic, and sustainable products remain that way throughout the entire supply chain. By increasing traceability and transparency in the food supply chain, we hope to increase food safety and food fraud.

How it works for farmers

Unique ID

Each item will have a unique ID that can be used to track its journey.

Track

Integrated with our mobile app, you can view exactly where your produce is in the supply chain in real time.

Remain authentic

With our blockchain technology, we can authenticate food origin and prevent fraud.

Impact

With your help, we can fullfil our mission to make the world a more transparent place.

Sustainable, Organic, and Safe

By giving you the tools to follow the journey your food takes, from farm to fork, you can be sure that what you are consuming is organic, sustainable, and safe.

How it works for users

Order

Using our app, you can see which products will be coming to your local area and place an order.

Track

You can track your order and know exactly where it is at any given time.

Free Delivery

Cut out all the unnecessary travel and get your order delivered straight to your door for free!

Impact

Help us and our dedicated farmers to make the world a more transparent place.
